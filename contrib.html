Giving back

Charity

We love our jobs and feel blessed to do what we love every day. So it's only natural we'd want to share that blessing with as many people as we can. Giving to worthy, game-changing charities is the best part of our company! Every year we donate to our selected charity.

This year's charity (again) is a wonderful organization whose mission hits close to home:

Open Source

We are huge advocates of information sharing and think open source is one of the best ways to achieve it. Open source gives us and others a huge productivity boost and it's only decent to try to give back where you can. In that spirit we make an effort to actively work on open source projects.

Have a cool open source project you could use our help with? Give us a shout.
